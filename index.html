What it means: Reading the calendar, not any announcement: we are 20 days from Preview Night, Comic-Con has locked its guest list and switched into countdown mode, and Aspen still has no listed panel, booth, or Ekos exclusive for 2026. Aspen ran a dedicated Ekos panel at SDCC 2025, so the realistic next domino is the full programming schedule (which historically drops in early to mid July) revealing an Aspen panel time, or an exclusive reveal surfacing first on @AspenComics or @Mythos_Studios. None of that is confirmed yet, so treat this as us watching the runway, not a news beat.
For the feed: A "20 days out" countdown post that leans into Comic-Con's own countdown energy: "Comic-Con just locked its final guest list. 20 days to Preview Night. What do you most want from Ekos at the show?" Run it as a story poll (panel / new reveal / exclusive print / creator signing) so the page rides the con momentum without claiming any unconfirmed Aspen news.
A Michael Turner art book is still backable, and the Ekos family is part of it Press/Unconfirmed
The standing Press item is Clover Press's "The DC Art of Michael Turner" hardcover. Per earlier reporting (June 16), Ekos writer and executive producer Geoff Johns contributed an introduction and Aspen president Frank Mastromauro spoke about the collaboration. This is a Clover Press book, not an Aspen or Ekos release, so the details stay Press/Unconfirmed. What is Confirmed and still current: the @AspenComics X bio continues to point followers to the project ("DC ART OF MICHAEL TURNER now live on Kickstarter").
What it means: As analysis, not Ekos news: Turner's legacy is the heart of the Ekos universe, and the people around Ekos are publicly tied to this book. With the campaign still open, there is a respectful, on-brand window to celebrate his artistry, kept clearly separate from any Ekos product so followers are not misled about who is publishing it.
For the feed: A respectful tribute carousel of Turner's DC covers, captioned "Michael Turner's DC artistry is getting a hardcover tribute from Clover Press," crediting the publisher clearly. Add a story link sticker to the Kickstarter while it is still live for fans who want to back it.
