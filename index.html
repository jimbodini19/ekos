The News

A new Michael Turner art book is on the way, and the Ekos family is part of it Press/Unconfirmed
Clover Press has announced "The DC Art of Michael Turner," a hardcover celebrating Turner's DC work, now live on Kickstarter. Per the report, Ekos writer and executive producer Geoff Johns contributed an introduction and Aspen Comics president Frank Mastromauro spoke about the collaboration. This is a Clover Press book, not an Aspen or Ekos release, so the details are Press/Unconfirmed. What is Confirmed: the @AspenComics X bio now points followers to the project ("DC ART OF MICHAEL TURNER now live on Kickstarter").
What it means: Reading this as analysis only, not as Ekos news: Turner's legacy is the emotional core of the Ekos universe, and the people around Ekos (Johns, Mastromauro) are publicly tied to this book. For a fan page that honors Turner, this is a respectful, on-brand moment to celebrate his artistry, separate from any Ekos product cycle. Worth keeping clear that this is a Clover Press project so followers are not led to think it is an Aspen or Ekos item.
For the feed: A respectful tribute carousel of Turner's DC covers with a caption like "Michael Turner's DC artistry is getting a hardcover tribute from Clover Press." Credit the publisher clearly and avoid implying any Aspen or Ekos involvement beyond what is reported. Pair with a story link sticker to the Kickstarter for fans who want to back it.
